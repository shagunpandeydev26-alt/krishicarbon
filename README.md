> Turn Sustainable Farming into Verified Climate Income.

KrishiCarbon Exchange is a scalable, AI-powered digital infrastructure platform designed to enable small and marginal farmers to monetize sustainable farming practices through satellite-based carbon estimation and blockchain-ready carbon credit trading.

The platform implements a secure multi-stakeholder ecosystem powered by Firebase Authentication, Firestore database, Node.js microservices, and role-based access control (RBAC).

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Over 100 million small farmers in India unknowingly practice climate-positive farming but cannot monetize carbon credits due to complex verification systems designed for large corporations.

KrishiCarbon Exchange simplifies the process by:

- Using AI-powered satellite carbon estimation
- Providing secure digital identity via Firebase
- Enabling transparent carbon marketplaces
- Supporting government-scale analytics

---

# 🎯 Multi-Stakeholder Ecosystem

The platform supports three distinct stakeholder roles:

| Role | Description | Key Capabilities |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| 🌾 Farmer | Small & marginal farmers | Register land, submit sustainable practices, generate carbon credits |
| 🏢 Corporate Buyer | ESG-driven companies | Purchase verified carbon credits, track ESG metrics |
| 🏛 Government/Admin | Regulatory authorities | Monitor compliance, detect fraud, analyze impact |
